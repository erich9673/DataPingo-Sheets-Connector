‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I get my Slack webhook URL?
A: Follow the step-by-step instructions in the app's Slack Configuration section. You'll need to create a Slack app and enable incoming webhooks.
Q: What's the minimum monitoring frequency?
A: You can monitor as frequently as every 30 seconds, but we recommend 5 minutes for most use cases to avoid hitting API limits.
Q: Why am I not receiving notifications?
A: Check that your Slack webhook URL is correct, your Google Sheets permissions are granted, and your monitoring conditions are properly configured.
Q: Can I monitor multiple spreadsheets?
A: Yes! You can set up multiple monitoring jobs for different spreadsheets and ranges.
Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes. We use OAuth 2.0 for secure access, encrypt all communications, and don't permanently store your spreadsheet data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Q: How do I stop monitoring?
A: Use the "Stop All Jobs" button in the Active Monitoring Jobs section, or revoke access via your Google Account settings.

üîß Troubleshooting

Authentication Issues

  • Ensure you're using the correct Google account
  • Check that Google Sheets API access is enabled
  • Try signing out and signing in again

Notification Issues

  • Verify your Slack webhook URL is correct
  • Test the webhook URL using the "Test Slack Connection" button
  • Check your Slack channel permissions

Monitoring Issues

  • Ensure the cell range format is correct (e.g., A1:C10)
  • Check that the spreadsheet is accessible to your Google account
  • Verify monitoring conditions are properly set
